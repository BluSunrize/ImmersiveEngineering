Storage Barrels
Doesn't roll
<&wooden_barrel>Wooden Barrels are simple containers that can store up to 12 buckets of liquid, but not gasses or hot fluids.<br>
Right-clicking the vertical sides of the barrel with the Engineer's Hammer will configure them.<np>
<&metal_barrel>Metal Barrels are identical to their wooden counterparts, other than the material of their construction.<br>
Metal barrels may store hot fluids as well as gasses, and their automatic output can be disabled by a redstone signal.
